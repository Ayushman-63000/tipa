Recyclable or compostable?
Glad you asked!

Despite the popularity of recyclables, most plastics never make it to recycling, especially flexible ones. In the US, only 1% of flexible packaging is recycled, with under 4% in Europe. Conversely, compostable packaging offers a more effective path to sustainability by breaking down into soil-enriching nutrients, leaving zero waste behind. Encouragingly, over 37 million Americans and 600 cities now participate in food waste and compostable packaging programs, with community composting expanding nationwide.

Circular Solution for flexible packaging

Most flexible packaging still follows the outdated make-take-dispose model. Even if sent for recycling, only 3% of packaging is actually recycled due to food contamination or because of multiple layers. TIPA offers a true circular solution: our packaging turns into compost within 6-12 months, just like organic waste.

Dispose of your packaging easily

Encourage your customers to dispose of their TIPA’s compostable packaging just like they dispose of food -Into the food waste stream or their home compost bin.
